Try this recipe for a special dinner, this spiced lamb is mild thanks to the fact that it is marinated in spices and Greek style yoghurt FAGE Total.

Ingredients:
  • 1 rack of lamb
  • olive oils, (a little)
  • Salt
  • ground black peppers
  • 1 1/8 cups of Greek style yoghurt Fage® Total 0%, (for the marinade)
  • 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped (for the marinade)
  • 1/2 onions, finely chopped (for the marinade)
  • 1 teaspoon chili flake
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 2 teaspoons rosemary, minced
Steps:
  1. Beat all the marinade ingredients in a large bowl for 4 minutes.
  2. Place the lamb and season.
  3. Rub the lamb with the FAGE® Total 0% Greek Style Yoghurt marinade on both sides.
  4. Place in a freezer bag and place with the marinade, refrigerate up to 12 hours.
  5. To cook: Preheat the oven to 350ºF and heat the olive oil in a pan or tray.
  6. Seal the lamb and let it roast in the oven for 2 and a half hours.
