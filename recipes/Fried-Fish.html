Fish

This is a delicious dish, it combines fresh vegetables with the fresh taste of the sea. Fried fish.

In this case we can see perfectly cooked fish with and a little lemon.

Ingredients:
  • Mojarra fish
  • Cucumber
  • Lemon
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Lemon
Steps:
  1. Wash all ingredients.
  2. Heating oil in a pan.
  3. Re-roll the cucumber and slice the lemons.
  4. Place the fish in the hot oil and fry it with salt.
  5. Serve the fish accompanied by cucumber with a touch of lemon.
